About Me

I am a combat veteran and instructor with experience serving in Israel and defending Ukraine since 2022. I specialize in preparing civilians and military personnel to act effectively under real threats: survival, self-defense (Krav Maga), field tactics, guerrilla warfare, and basic medical aid.

I have trained hundreds of people, including defense forces units. For a long time, I provided free training to military personnel, for which I have official thanks from combat brigades and commanders.

I create original courses based on real combat and volunteer experience so everyone can learn to act confidently, decisively, and without panic when it really matters.
