About Me

I am a combat veteran and instructor with experience serving in Israel and defending Ukraine since 2022. Specialized in training civilians and military personnel for real threat situations: survival, self-defense (Krav Maga), field tactics, guerrilla warfare, and basic first aid.

I have trained hundreds of people, including defense forces units. Provided free training for military for extended periods, for which I have official commendations from combat brigades and commanders.

I create original courses based on real combat and volunteer experience so everyone can learn to act confidently, decisively, and without panic when it really matters.
